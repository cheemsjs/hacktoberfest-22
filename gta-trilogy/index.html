Grand Theft Auto III

Grand Theft Auto III is a 2001 action-adventure videogame developed by Rockstar North (then known as DMA Design) and published by Rockstar Games. Set in the fictional Liberty City in 2001, the game's narrative follows the adventures of Claude, a silent criminal who was betrayed by his girlfriend Catalina during a bank heist. Claude begins to work for different factions in the crime-riddled Liberty City, including the Mafia, Yakuza and corrupted figures. Set in an open-world environment, Grand Theft Auto III is the first game in the series to be presented in a fully 3D world

Grand Theft Auto Vice City

Grand Theft Auto: Vice City is a video game developed by Rockstar North. GTA Vice City is set in Vice City, Florida, in 1986, and concerns the exploits of protagonist Tommy Vercetti as he rises to become Vice City's major criminal power.

Grand Theft Auto San Andreas

Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas is a Rockstar North-developed video game. set in the fictional state of San Andreas. By far the largest and most complex playing field of any 3D Universe game, San Andreas consists of three fully-realized cities: Los Santos, San Fierro and Las Venturas, and includes vast expanses of countryside, forests, a desert, and even a mountain and many hills. The game is centered around the return of Carl Johnson (also known as CJ) to his gang (the Grove Street Families), and their subsequent return to power.
